Community Guidelines

Please follow these guidelines to help maintain a positive and helpful community environment.

General Guidelines

  • Be respectful and courteous to all community members
  • Use clear, descriptive titles for your posts
  • Use clear, descriptive titles for your posts
  • Provide accurate and truthful information
  • Keep posts relevant to the community
  • No spam, advertising, or promotional content

Content Standards

  • No offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate language
  • No personal attacks or harassment
  • Respect privacy - don't share personal information of others
  • No illegal activities or content
  • Keep discussions constructive and helpful

Lost & Found

  • Include clear descriptions and contact information
  • Remove posts once items are found/returned
  • Be cautious when meeting strangers
  • Verify ownership before returning valuable items

Events

  • Include date, time, and location details
  • Update or remove posts for cancelled events
  • Ensure events comply with community guidelines
  • Provide contact information for questions

Urgent Notices

  • Only use for genuine emergencies or urgent matters
  • Provide clear, actionable information
  • Include contact details for immediate response
  • Update posts with new information as needed

Enforcement

Posts that violate these guidelines may be removed without notice. Repeated violations may result in restricted posting privileges.

If you see content that violates these guidelines, please use the Contact/Feedback section to report it to the moderators.

Thank you for helping to keep our community notice board a safe and helpful space for everyone!
